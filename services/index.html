Overview

Care designed around how you live and see.

We provide full-scope optometric care for children, adults, and families, with services focused on clear vision, long-term eye health, and personalized treatment for daily life.

Eye Exams

Eye exams beyond a prescription.

Our comprehensive exams evaluate both vision and overall eye health. We assess how well you see, examine the eyes inside and out, and recommend the right next step for clearer, more comfortable vision.

  • Routine vision and prescription evaluation
  • External and internal eye health assessment
  • Early detection of potential eye disease
  • Recommendations tailored to daily comfort and clarity
Children

Pediatric care for growing eyes.

Children’s vision can change quickly. Regular pediatric eye exams help monitor development, support learning, and identify concerns early, when treatment can make the greatest difference.

Contact Lenses

Contact lens care with confidence.

Whether you are new to contacts or looking for a better fit, we provide exams, fittings, and follow-up guidance to help you wear lenses safely and comfortably.

Dry Eye

Dry eye treatment, tailored to you.

Dry eye can happen when the eyes do not produce enough tears or when tears evaporate too quickly. We identify the cause first, then create a treatment plan designed to improve comfort and long-term eye surface health.

Ocular Disease

Early detection. Ongoing care.

Many eye conditions develop quietly at first. We focus on early detection, accurate diagnosis, and personalized management for glaucoma, dry eye disease, macular degeneration, diabetic eye disease, infection, and inflammation.

Myopia

Myopia management for long-term vision.

Myopia is increasingly common in children and young adults. We offer myopia management strategies designed to slow progression and support healthier long-term visual development.

Urgent Care

Prompt care for urgent eye concerns.

Eye emergencies can include scratches, foreign objects, redness, pain, light-related injury, blunt trauma, or sudden vision changes. If you are experiencing an urgent eye issue, contact our office as soon as possible.

Surgical Support

LASIK and cataract care, closely guided.

From evaluation to post-operative follow-up, we work closely with trusted surgeons to support a smooth LASIK or cataract surgery experience with convenient, personalized care.
