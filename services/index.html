Overview

Care designed around how you live and see.

We provide full-scope optometric care for children, adults, and families, with services focused on clear vision, long-term eye health, and personalized treatment for daily life.

Comprehensive Exams

Eye exams that look beyond a prescription.

Our comprehensive eye exams evaluate both vision and overall eye health. We examine the eyes externally and internally for signs of disease, then test your vision in a range of ways to better understand what you need.

  • Routine vision and prescription evaluation
  • External and internal eye health assessment
  • Early detection of potential eye disease
  • Personalized recommendations for daily comfort and clarity
Children

Pediatric eye care that supports learning and growth.

Children’s eyes can change quickly as they grow. Regular pediatric eye exams help monitor development, support school performance, and identify vision concerns early, when treatment can make the biggest difference.

Contact Lenses

Confident contact lens care, from first exam to follow-up.

If you are new to contact lenses, we make the process approachable and comfortable. We provide contact lens exams, fittings, and follow-up guidance to help you wear lenses safely and confidently.

Dry Eye

Dry eye treatment tailored to the way your eyes function.

Dry eye can happen when the eyes do not produce enough tears or when tears evaporate too quickly. We first identify the specific type of dry eye, then create a personalized treatment plan designed to improve comfort, daily function, and long-term eye surface health.

Ocular Disease

Early detection and ongoing care for eye disease.

Many eye conditions develop without obvious symptoms at first. Our ocular disease management focuses on early detection, accurate diagnosis, and personalized treatment for conditions such as glaucoma, dry eye disease, macular degeneration, diabetic eye disease, infections, and inflammation.

Myopia

Myopia management for long-term visual health.

Myopia is increasingly common in children and young adults. We offer myopia management strategies to help slow progression and support healthier long-term vision development.

Urgent Care

Prompt attention for urgent eye concerns.

Eye emergencies can include trauma, cuts, scratches, foreign objects, chemical exposure, burns, light-related injury, and blunt injury to the eye or eyelid. If you are experiencing an urgent eye issue, contact our office as soon as possible.

Surgical Support

LASIK and cataract co-management with close follow-through.

From your initial evaluation to post-operative care, we work closely with trusted surgeons to support a smooth and successful LASIK or cataract surgery experience. Follow-up care in our office helps keep the process convenient, personal, and closely monitored.
