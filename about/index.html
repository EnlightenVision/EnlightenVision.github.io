Company

A different kind of eye care.

At EnlightenVision Optometry, we believe every patient deserves care that feels thoughtful, calm, and personal. From comprehensive eye exams to personalized eyewear, every step is designed to support both your vision and your long-term eye health.

Personalized Solutions

Because no two eyes — and no two lives — are exactly the same.

We take the time to understand how you work, read, drive, and experience the world. That means your prescription, lens recommendations, and vision plan are tailored to your real life, not just your numbers.

  • Comprehensive eye examinations
  • Contact lens fittings
  • Eyewear tailored to your lifestyle
  • Long-term preventive eye health care

Comfort in Every Step

A calmer, more human clinical experience.

We believe eye care should feel welcoming, not rushed. Our environment is modern, warm, and carefully designed to help patients feel informed, respected, and at ease.

Doctors

Experienced care, guided by expertise.

At EnlightenVision Optometry, we are proud to have experienced doctors and skilled professionals dedicated to providing authoritative, high-quality vision care.

Our lead optometrist, Dr. Jay Jiang, has more than 20 years of professional experience and holds advanced credentials including Opt, M.S., Ph.D. With deep clinical knowledge and a patient-centered approach, Dr. Jiang is committed to delivering precise, trusted, and personalized care for every patient.

Every recommendation is made with clarity, integrity, and attention to long-term eye health — so patients feel confident not only in their vision, but also in the care behind it.

Staffs

A dedicated team behind every visit.

Beyond our clinical expertise, our staff works closely with every patient to create an experience that feels welcoming, efficient, and reassuring.

From appointment coordination to frame selection and day-to-day patient support, our team is committed to making each visit smooth, attentive, and comfortable from beginning to end.

Together, our doctors and staff strive to provide not only professional excellence, but also the warmth and reliability that patients can trust.

Our Philosophy

See clearly. Live fully.

EnlightenVision is about more than prescriptions. It’s about helping people feel confident in how they see, how they move through the world, and how they care for their health over time.
