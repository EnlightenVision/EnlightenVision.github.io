Services

Services

At EnlightenVision Optometry, we are committed to providing quality eye care for you and your family.

Myopia Management

Myopia is increasingly common, especially among children and young adults. We provide personalized strategies to help manage progression and support healthier long-term vision.

Comprehensive Eye Exams

Our comprehensive eye exams evaluate both your vision and your eye health. We examine the eyes inside and out, check prescription needs, and look for early signs of disease.

Pediatric Eyecare

Children’s eyes can change quickly as they grow. Regular pediatric eye exams help support healthy development, learning, and long-term visual comfort.

Contact Lens Exam

A contact lens exam helps determine the best lens fit, prescription, and wearing plan for your lifestyle, comfort, and eye health.

Pre / Post LASIK Care

We provide pre-operative evaluations and post-operative follow-up for LASIK patients, helping ensure clarity, safety, and continuity of care.

Urgent Eye Care

If you are experiencing redness, pain, irritation, scratches, foreign objects, or other eye emergencies, we provide prompt care when you need it most.

Book an Appointment

Whether you need a routine eye exam, specialized care, or urgent attention, our team is here to help you see clearly and comfortably.
